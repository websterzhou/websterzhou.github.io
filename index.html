Data exploration of Airbnb data and simple price analysis

Discussing the existence of conditional volatility in U.S. house prices

In this short report, I provide graphical and statistical arguments in an investigation into the existence of conditional heteroscedasticity in U.S. house price. I try to first model the underlying series and then model a ARCH(1) process as the conditional variance.

Time series, Volatility modelling

ML modelling of stock returns

In this project, I used OLS, lasso, adaptive lasso, elastic net, adaptive elastic net, and random forest models to predict stock returns. I then compared the predictive power of each model using the confusion matrix and ROC curves.

Machine learning, model evaluation

Propensity score matching

This study investigates the effect of having a first-born child on a married individual's personal wellbeing in the UK. The Propensity Score Matching method is used to control for potential endogeneity in the treatment decision.
